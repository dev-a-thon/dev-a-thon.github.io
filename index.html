About MACE

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by Mar Athanasius College Association, Aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was first in Asia under Christian Management. It was also the first engineering college to get affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It now has six full fledged departments

About Takshak

We at MACE set apart a time each year to celebrate the very realm of technology that has kept us thriving. Takshak, the annual technical festival of MACE, will witness each department pay their tribute to the science that is engineering. We believe that there is much more on Earth that we are yet to discover, than what we know right now, and Takshak stands testimony to this. Undoubtedly one of the most reputeable technical festivals in the state, Takshak is what would make a tech-savvy student keep heaven waiting. With competitions, seminars, exhibitions and much more, Takshak provides the best students in the state to crisscross, fight, triumph, celebrate, rejoice, and most importantly, discover themselves in the world around them.

Dev-a-thon

Dev-a-thon is a 24 hour hackathon, to be conducted as part of Takshak- the techfest of Mar Athanasius College Of Engineering, Kothamangalam which is open to all kinds of students. This is a platform provided to aspiring developers to create a software/hardware based product with vibrant ideas, which will be scrutinized by a panel of skillful judges. The judging will be based on various criteria like technology used, creativity and innovation. Various fun activities are also being planned to make the long 24 hours of work a little less stressful. Providing a comfortable and motivational vibe for the participants is one of the important factors to be focussed, for which we are organising activities and other facilities in order to increase their enthusiasm to complete their products in time.
